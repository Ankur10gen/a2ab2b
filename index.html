Agents acting on behalf of businesses

Agentic AI has rapidly evolved from concept to reality in recent months.

Pioneering protocols like Anthropic's Model Context Protocol (MCP) and Google's Agent-to-Agent Protocol (A2A) have laid the foundation for a new paradigm in business communication.

Soon, enterprise software platforms will deploy their own autonomous agents, transforming traditional business relationships.

The future of Business-to-Business (B2B) is becoming Agent-to-Agent (A2A) — intelligent entities negotiating and collaborating on behalf of the organizations they represent.

A2AB2B embodies this vision — where autonomous agents become the primary interface between businesses, with limitless possibilities ahead.
